About Me

Hello! I'm Yuvan, a final-year MCA student at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. With a strong engineering foundation established during my BSc in IT at GRD College of Science, I specialize in building practical, high-impact mobile and intelligence solutions.

I focus intensely on the intersection of Mobile Development and Machine Learning. I build intelligent applications that execute localized ML model inference (using TensorFlow Lite) directly on mobile devices—ensuring complete user data privacy and removing latency constraints.
